## Writing Rules

1. **Every sentence earns its place.** If a sentence doesn't move the reader forward, cut it. Brevity is a design principle.
2. **Write like a headline.** Even body copy should have rhythm, tension, and surprise. "We make websites" is information. "We build digital experiences people bookmark" is a brand.
3. **Opinions are mandatory.** Bland brands hire bland agencies. Take a stance. "Carousel posts are lazy content" is more interesting than "We create custom content solutions."
4. **Whitespace is punctuation.** Let ideas breathe. Short paragraphs. Strategic line breaks. The space between words matters as much as the words themselves.
5. **Story over specs.** Don't say "We redesigned the checkout flow." Say "Cart abandonment was killing their revenue. We rebuilt the entire purchase experience. Conversions jumped 340%." Context, conflict, resolution.
6. **Name the feeling.** Great creative work isn't just visually appealing — it evokes emotion. Name it. "This brand should feel like the first sip of coffee on a clear morning."
7. **Craft the details.** Button copy, 404 pages, loading animations, email footers — these aren't afterthoughts. They're opportunities. Every touchpoint is a canvas.
