# Contracts Guide

## Essential Contracts by Stage

| Stage | Must Have | Should Have |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Pre-seed | Co-founder agreement, IP assignment | NDA template |
| Seed | + Employment agreements, contractor agreements | + Advisor agreements (FAST) |
| Revenue | + Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, DPA | + MSA, SLA |
| Series A+ | + Stock option plan, board consents | + D&O insurance |

## Co-Founder Agreement

### Must Include
- **Equity split:** Percentage each founder owns
- **Vesting:** 4 years, 1-year cliff (standard). Protects everyone if someone leaves early
- **Roles:** Who does what (clear responsibilities)
- **IP assignment:** All IP belongs to the company, not individuals
- **Decision-making:** How decisions are made (voting, CEO authority)
- **What if someone leaves:** Buyback rights, accelerated vesting triggers
- **Non-compete:** Reasonable scope and duration
- **Conflict resolution:** Mediation before litigation

### Common Mistakes
- No vesting (one founder leaves after 3 months with 50%)
- No IP assignment (founder claims ownership of the code)
- Unclear decision-making (deadlocks with no resolution mechanism)
- No discussion of exit scenarios

## Contractor Agreement

### Key Clauses
- Scope of work (deliverables, timeline)
- Payment terms (hourly/fixed, schedule, net terms)
- IP assignment (work product belongs to company)
- Confidentiality (NDA built in)
- Termination (notice period, payment for completed work)
- Independent contractor status (not employee — define clearly)

## Employment Agreement

### Key Clauses
- Position and responsibilities
- Compensation (salary, equity, benefits)
- At-will employment (in the US)
- Invention assignment (all work belongs to company)
- Confidentiality and non-disclosure
- Non-solicitation (can't recruit colleagues or customers)
- Non-compete (limited duration and scope — not enforceable everywhere)

## Advisor Agreement (FAST Standard)

| Involvement Level | Idea Stage | Development | Revenue |
|-------------------|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Standard (5 hrs/month) | 0.25% | 0.15% | 0.10% |
| Strategic (10 hrs/month) | 0.50% | 0.35% | 0.25% |
| Expert (20 hrs/month) | 1.00% | 0.60% | 0.50% |

Standard: 2-year vesting, no cliff.

## Red Flags in Contracts
- No termination clause
- Unlimited liability
- Automatic renewal without notice
- Non-compete that's too broad (geography, duration)
- IP assignment to the other party (in contracts where you're the vendor)
- Exclusive rights without performance guarantees
- Arbitration in unfavorable jurisdiction
