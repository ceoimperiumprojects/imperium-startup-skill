# Sales Triggers Reference (137 Triggers)

## Why Signal-Based Selling Works

| Outreach Type | Reply Rate |
|---------------|------------|
| Cold outreach (no signal) | 6-8% |
| Signal-based outreach | 18-22% |
| Multi-signal stacked | 35-40% |

Signal-based outreach delivers 3-4x higher contract values. Don't spray and pray — target people experiencing triggers that make your solution relevant NOW.

## 6 Core Buying Signals (Ranked by Purchase Correlation)

### 1. Former Customers & Alumni Users (Highest)
- Trust already established, known playbook
- Query: Previous users of your product at new companies
- Timing: Immediately upon detection
- Reply rate: 25-30%

### 2. New Leadership (≤90 days)
- New execs have mandate for early wins, vendor amnesty period
- Query: LinkedIn job changes, press releases
- Timing: Days 14-45 (peak window)
- Reply rate: 8-12%

### 3. High-Intent Website & Content
- Pricing page visits, competitor comparisons, demo page
- Query: Website visitor tracking, content downloads
- Timing: Within 24-48 hours
- Reply rate: 25-30% (they know you)

### 4. Tech Stack Change
- Active change project signals openness, fresh pain from transition
- Query: BuiltWith, job postings mentioning new tools
- Timing: 1-2 weeks after detection

### 5. Expansion (Raise, New Region/Product)
- Board targets create urgency, scale pain emerges
- Query: Crunchbase, press releases, job postings
- Timing: 2-4 weeks after announcement

### 6. Hiring or Downsizing
- Hiring = ramp pressure. Downsizing = do-more-with-less mandate
- Query: LinkedIn company growth, layoff news
- Timing: 1-2 weeks after pattern detected

## Outbound Triggers by Category

### Company Signals
- Just raised funding
- IPO filing
- Acquisition/merger
- Office expansion / new location
- Product launch
- Regulatory filing
- Partnership announcement
- Rebranding
- Executive departure
- Revenue milestone
- Customer milestone (100th, 1000th)

### Hiring Signals
- Hiring for role your product replaces
- Hiring for role that uses your product
- Job posting mentions competitor tool
- Hiring surge (10+ roles in 30 days)
- Specific skills in job descriptions
- Uncommon job titles (signals priority)
- Hiring SDRs (sales tools opportunity)

### Technology Signals
- Adopted new tool (complementary to yours)
- Dropped competitor product
- Technology migration in progress
- New tech stack mentioned in job postings
- GitHub activity changes
- Infrastructure changes detected

### Growth/Scale Signals
- Revenue growth (press/Crunchbase)
- Employee count increase
- New market entry
- International expansion
- New product line
- Partnership expansion

### Pain Signals
- Negative reviews on G2/Capterra
- Customer complaints on social media
- Security incident
- Service outage
- Employee complaints on Glassdoor
- Layoffs (do more with less)
- Executive turnover (instability)

### Industry/Timing Signals
- Regulation change affecting them
- Industry trend shift
- Competitor raised funding (pressure)
- End of fiscal quarter (budget spending)
- Contract renewal timing
- Budget season
- Board meeting prep period

### Personal Signals
- Job change (new role = new vendors)
- Promotion
- Work anniversary
- Published content about your problem space
- Conference speaking engagement
- Podcast appearance
- LinkedIn post about relevant topic
- Social media activity about pain point

### Engagement Signals
- Visited your website (multiple pages)
- Downloaded content
- Attended webinar
- Opened emails repeatedly
- Clicked email links
- Viewed pricing page
- Viewed competitor comparison page
- Started but didn't complete signup

## Trigger Response Framework

```
First Line: Reference the specific trigger (proves research)
Second Line: Connect to relevant pain/opportunity
Third Line: Your relevance (one sentence)
Fourth Line: Soft CTA
```

**Priority:** Hand-raisers > High-intent > Company signals > Personal signals > Industry signals
