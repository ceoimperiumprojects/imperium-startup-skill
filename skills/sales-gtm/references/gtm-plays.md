# 11 GTM Plays

## Play 1: New Team Members
**Signal:** Company added team members
**Timing:** 2-4 weeks after hire
**Approach:** Reference new hire by name, shows attention to their growth
**Template:** "Noticed you just brought {{name}} on as {{role}}. When teams grow, {{problem}} usually gets worse before it gets better."

## Play 2: Skills-Targeting
**Signal:** LinkedIn skills match your product domain
**Timing:** Ongoing
**Approach:** Target specific LinkedIn skills (not just titles). Clay extracts skills from profiles.
**Example:** Salesforce skill → Salesforce add-ons. Python skill → Developer tools.
**Template:** "Saw {{skill}} on your profile. Most people with that expertise run into {{problem}}."

## Play 3: Role-Targeting
**Signal:** Uncommon job title exists at company
**Timing:** Ongoing
**Approach:** Uncommon titles signal budget and priority.
**Example:** "RevOps Manager" → RevOps is a priority. "Growth Engineer" → Technical growth team exists.
**Template:** "Not many companies have a dedicated {{title}} yet. Usually means {{inference}}."

## Play 4: Industry Research
**Signal:** Create your own signal via surveys/research
**Timing:** Value-first (before any pitch)
**Approach:** Survey ICP experts, publish findings, use as conversation opener.
**Template:** "We surveyed 50 {{role}}s about {{topic}}. [Surprising finding]. Curious how {{company}} compares?"

## Play 5: Resources for ICs (Evergreen)
**Signal:** New ICs (SDRs, AEs, CSMs) starting roles
**Timing:** Evergreen — always running
**Approach:** Automated campaign sending useful resources to new individual contributors. Long-term brand building.
**Template:** "Welcome to the {{role}} world! Here are 3 resources I wish I had on day one: [link]"

## Play 6: Leaving Employees
**Signal:** Team member departed
**Timing:** 1-2 weeks after departure
**Approach:** Position around the coverage gap.
**Template:** "Saw {{name}} left {{company}}. That usually creates a gap in {{function}}. We help teams maintain {{capability}} during transitions."

## Play 7: No Dedicated Role
**Signal:** Missing key title at the company
**Timing:** Ongoing
**Approach:** No "Content Manager" + $5M+ revenue = content gap opportunity.
**Template:** "Noticed {{company}} doesn't have a dedicated {{role}} yet. At your stage, most companies are doing {{workaround}}. We can handle {{function}} for you."

## Play 8: Bad Reviews Targeting
**Signal:** Negative G2/Capterra reviews of competitor
**Timing:** Within days of review
**Approach:** Scrape negative reviews, reference their pain, offer alternative.
**Template:** "Saw some recent feedback about {{competitor}} on G2. The {{specific_complaint}} seems to be a common theme. We built {{product}} specifically to solve that."

## Play 9: AI-Generated Ideas
**Signal:** None needed — create value with personalized ideas
**Timing:** Ongoing
**Approach:** Generate 2 relevant ideas for the prospect + your offer as #3 (with humor).
**Template:** "3 ideas for {{company}}: 1) {{idea_1}} 2) {{idea_2}} 3) Talk to us about {{your_value}}. (Ok, #3 is self-serving, but 1 and 2 are real.)"

## Play 10: ServiceBell Allbound (Real-time)
**Signal:** Visitor hits your website (especially pricing page)
**Timing:** Real-time (within minutes)
**Approach:** Visitor hits pricing → SDR calls in 2 min → email if no answer → LinkedIn next day.
**Sequence:** Phone (2 min) → Email (same day) → LinkedIn (next day)

## Play 11: Inbound Followers
**Signal:** New LinkedIn follower
**Timing:** 24-48 hours
**Approach:** Monitor daily, filter to ICP via Clay, outreach within 24-48h.
**Template:** "Thanks for the follow! Noticed you're in {{industry}}. We've been working with teams like {{company}} on {{problem}}."

## Play Selection Guide

| ACV | Best Plays | Why |
|-----|-----------|-----|
| <$5K | 5 (Evergreen), 9 (AI Ideas), 11 (Followers) | Volume-based, lower touch |
| $5-30K | 1 (New Team), 2 (Skills), 8 (Bad Reviews) | Signal-based, medium touch |
| $30K+ | 4 (Research), 6 (Departures), 7 (No Role) | Account-based, high touch |

## Play Execution in Clay

For each play:
1. Set up Clay table with signal source
2. Enrich with company/contact data
3. Apply qualification filters (ICP match, company size)
4. Generate personalized first line using AI
5. Push to sequence tool (Instantly, Smartlead)
6. Track results by play
