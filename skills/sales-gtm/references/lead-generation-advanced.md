# Lead Generation — Advanced

The 4 core lead generation methods, warm/cold frameworks, and content-led growth. Distilled from Alex Hormozi's "$100M Leads" (281 chunks in knowledge base).

## The Lead Generation Framework

Every business gets leads from exactly 4 sources:

```
                    ┌─────────────────────┐
                    │     YOUR LEADS      │
                    └──────────┬──────────┘
          ┌───────────┬───────┴───────┬────────────┐
    ┌─────┴─────┐ ┌───┴────┐  ┌──────┴──────┐ ┌───┴────┐
    │   Warm    │ │  Cold  │  │   Content   │ │  Paid  │
    │ Outreach  │ │Outreach│  │  (Free)     │ │  Ads   │
    └───────────┘ └────────┘  └─────────────┘ └────────┘
      1-to-1        1-to-1      1-to-many      1-to-many
      Free          Free        Free           Paid
```

**Rule:** Master ONE method before adding another. Most founders spread thin across all 4 and master none.

## Method 1: Warm Outreach

**Who:** People who already know you — friends, colleagues, LinkedIn connections, past clients, email contacts.

**Why start here:** Free, fast, highest conversion, and builds confidence.

### The Warm Outreach Script

```
1. Acknowledge the relationship
   "Hey [Name], hope you're doing well!"

2. Offer value (not a pitch)
   "I'm working on [thing] and looking for [X] people to try it
    free/at a discount in exchange for feedback."

3. Describe the result (not the product)
   "It helps [target customer] get [specific result]."

4. Make a soft ask
   "Know anyone who might be interested? Or would you want
    to try it yourself?"
```

**Key:** Position it as a favor to you (feedback), not a sales pitch. People love helping.

### Warm Outreach Targets

- Personal contacts (100-500 people)
- LinkedIn 1st connections
- Past colleagues and classmates
- People from communities/events you've attended
- Previous clients/customers

**Volume:** Reach out to 100 people in the first week. Expect 10-15 conversations, 3-5 customers.

## Method 2: Cold Outreach

**Who:** Strangers who match your ICP but don't know you exist.

**Why it works:** Unlimited scale, zero cost, fast feedback loop.

### Cold Outreach Formula

```
1. Personalized hook (proves you did research)
   "Saw your talk at SaaStr / noticed you're hiring for X"

2. Problem (that they recognize)
   "Most [role] at [company type] struggle with [problem]"

3. Bridge (not a feature dump)
   "We help [similar companies] achieve [result]"

4. CTA (one, low-friction)
   "Worth a 15-min call to see if it makes sense?"
```

### Volume Metrics

| Activity | Daily Target | Monthly | Expected Result |
|----------|-------------|---------|-----------------|
| Cold emails sent | 50-100 | 1,500-3,000 | 5-15% reply rate |
| LinkedIn connects | 20-50 | 600-1,500 | 10-30% accept |
| Cold calls | 20-50 | 600-1,500 | 2-5% meeting rate |
| Meetings booked | 2-5 | 60-150 | 20-40% close rate |

### Personalization at Scale

**3 levels of personalization:**

| Level | Effort | Reply Rate | How |
|-------|--------|------------|-----|
| **Generic** | Low | 1-3% | Same email to everyone |
| **Segment** | Medium | 5-10% | Personalized per ICP segment |
| **Individual** | High | 10-20% | Custom research per prospect |

**The sweet spot:** Segment-level personalization. Group prospects by role, industry, or trigger event. Customize the template per segment.

### Cold Outreach Infrastructure

**Deliverability checklist:**
- [ ] Separate sending domain (not your main domain)
- [ ] SPF, DKIM, DMARC configured
- [ ] Domain warmed for 2-4 weeks before sending
- [ ] Email validation on all addresses
- [ ] 50-100 emails/day per mailbox maximum
- [ ] Bounce rate <3%, spam rate <0.1%

## Method 3: Content (Free, 1-to-Many)

**Strategy:** Give away your best knowledge for free → build audience → audience becomes pipeline.

### Content Lead Gen Framework

```
Free Value → Trust → Audience → Demand → Leads
```

**The paradox:** "If I give it all away, why would they pay?" Because they pay for IMPLEMENTATION, not information. The free content proves you know what you're talking about.

### Content Types by Platform

| Platform | Best Content Type | Frequency | Lead Mechanism |
|----------|------------------|-----------|----------------|
| LinkedIn | Tactical posts, frameworks | 3-5x/week | DMs, comments |
| Twitter/X | Threads, insights | Daily | Link in bio, DMs |
| YouTube | How-to, case studies | 1-2x/week | Description links |
| Blog/SEO | Long-form guides | 2-4x/month | Lead magnets |
| Newsletter | Curated insights | Weekly | Replies, referrals |
| Podcast | Deep dives, interviews | Weekly | Show notes links |

### Lead Magnets

**Definition:** A free resource that solves a specific, narrow problem completely.

**Great lead magnets:**
- Checklists ("The 10-Point Landing Page Audit")
- Templates ("Cold Email Template Pack: 15 Proven Templates")
- Calculators ("SaaS Metrics Calculator")
- Mini-courses ("5-Day Email: From Idea to MVP")
- Free tools ("SEO Keyword Analyzer")

**Rules:**
- Solve ONE narrow problem completely (not a broad overview)
- Deliver immediate value (usable in <10 minutes)
- Make them think: "If the free stuff is this good, the paid must be incredible"
- Gate with email only (no 15-field forms)

## Method 4: Paid Ads

**Who:** Strangers you pay to reach.

**When to start:** AFTER you've validated offer and messaging with free methods.

### Paid Ads Decision Framework

| Channel | Best For | Min Budget | Time to Signal |
|---------|----------|------------|----------------|
| Google Search | High-intent, existing demand | $1K/mo | 2-4 weeks |
| Meta (FB/IG) | Broad B2C, awareness | $1K/mo | 2-4 weeks |
| LinkedIn | B2B, specific titles/companies | $3K/mo | 4-6 weeks |
| YouTube | Education, long consideration | $2K/mo | 4-8 weeks |
| TikTok | B2C, younger demographic | $500/mo | 1-2 weeks |

### Paid Ads Rules

1. **Test messaging with free content first** — Don't pay to test what organic can tell you
2. **One channel at a time** — Master it before adding another
3. **Track CAC religiously** — If CAC > LTV, stop immediately
4. **Creative > targeting** — Great ads with okay targeting beats okay ads with great targeting
5. **Retarget warm audiences first** — Website visitors, email list, social engagers (cheapest, highest conversion)

## Lead Gen Scaling Playbook

### Phase 1: Validation (Month 1-2)
- Warm outreach to 100+ contacts
- 10+ customer conversations
- Validate offer, pricing, messaging

### Phase 2: Systematic Growth (Month 3-4)
- Cold outreach: 50-100 emails/day
- Content: 3-5 posts/week on primary platform
- Build email list with lead magnet

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 5+)
- Add paid ads (retargeting first, then cold)
- Hire/automate cold outreach
- Launch referral program
- Add second content platform

## Lead Quality Scoring

Not all leads are equal. Score by:

| Signal | Points | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| Visited pricing page | +30 | High intent |
| Downloaded lead magnet | +15 | Problem-aware |
| Replied to cold email | +20 | Engaged |
| Matches ICP firmographics | +25 | Right fit |
| Multiple website visits | +20 | Researching |
| Referred by customer | +35 | Pre-qualified |
| Opened 3+ emails | +10 | Interested |

**Hot lead (80+):** Call within 24 hours.
**Warm lead (40-79):** Nurture sequence.
**Cold lead (<40):** Content drip, re-engage quarterly.
