## Follow-Up Templates (4)

### #27 Nooks SDR Follow-up (6-8% reply rate)
Add new angle + social proof. Reference original without re-pitching.

### #28 Value-Add Follow-up

### #29 Question Follow-up
Ask a genuine question about their challenge. No pitch.

## Email Writing Principles

1. Lead with pain, not features
2. Personalize to the PERSON, not just the company
3. Use specific social proof numbers ("47% increase" not "significant improvement")
4. Soft CTAs work better ("worth a look?" vs "book a call")
5. Humor reduces tension (use when natural)
6. New role signals peak at days 14-45
7. Value-first before asking
8. Under 100 words for first touch
9. One CTA per email
10. Subject lines: short, lowercase, curiosity-driven
