# Lead Qualification Frameworks

## BANT

| Criterion | Questions | Green Flag | Red Flag |
|-----------|-----------|------------|----------|
| **Budget** | "Is there a budget allocated?" | Active budget exists | "We don't have budget" |
| **Authority** | "Who makes this decision?" | Speaking to decision maker | "I'd have to check with..." |
| **Need** | "What problem are you solving?" | Urgent, specific pain | Vague, nice-to-have |
| **Timeline** | "When do you need a solution?" | This quarter | "Eventually" |

## MEDDIC (Enterprise)

| Element | Question | What You're Looking For |
|---------|----------|----------------------|
| **Metrics** | "What quantifiable goals?" | Specific numbers they need to hit |
| **Economic Buyer** | "Who controls the budget?" | Name and title of final approver |
| **Decision Criteria** | "How will you evaluate?" | Scorecard, RFP criteria, must-haves |
| **Decision Process** | "What's the buying process?" | Steps, approvals, timeline |
| **Identify Pain** | "What happens if you don't solve this?" | Consequence of inaction |
| **Champion** | "Who internally advocates?" | Someone who will sell for you internally |

## Lead Scoring Model

### Firmographic Fit (0-40 points)
- Company size matches ICP: +15
- Industry matches: +10
- Geography matches: +10
- Technology stack matches: +5

### Behavioral Signals (0-40 points)
- Visited pricing page: +15
- Downloaded content: +5
- Attended webinar: +10
- Multiple site visits in a week: +10
- Opened emails consistently: +5

### Engagement Quality (0-20 points)
- Responded to outreach: +10
- Asked specific product questions: +10
- Requested demo/trial: +20 (auto-qualify)

### Scoring Thresholds
- 70+: Hot lead — prioritize immediately
- 40-69: Warm lead — nurture with targeted content
- 20-39: Cool lead — add to drip campaign
- <20: Not qualified — remove or long-term nurture

## Disqualification Criteria

Immediately disqualify if:
- Company too small/large for your product
- No budget and no path to budget
- Problem doesn't match your solution
- Decision timeline > 12 months with no urgency
- Already locked into competitor contract with no exit
