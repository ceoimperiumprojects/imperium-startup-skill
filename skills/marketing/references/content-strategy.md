# Content Strategy

## Content Strategy Framework

### 1. Goals (What business outcome?)
- Awareness → Top of funnel traffic
- Leads → Email signups, free trial starts
- Revenue → Direct conversion content
- Retention → Help docs, onboarding, community
- Authority → Thought leadership, industry recognition

### 2. Audience (Who are we writing for?)
Map content to search intent:
- **Informational:** "How to..." "What is..." → Blog posts, guides
- **Navigational:** "[Your brand] pricing" → Product pages
- **Commercial:** "[Product] vs [Competitor]" → Comparison pages
- **Transactional:** "Buy [product]" → Landing pages, pricing

### 3. Content Types by Funnel Stage

| Stage | Content Type | Goal | Example |
|-------|-------------|------|---------|
| TOFU | Blog posts, guides, tools | Traffic, awareness | "10 Cold Email Templates That Work" |
| MOFU | Case studies, webinars, comparisons | Consideration | "[Product] vs [Competitor]" |
| BOFU | Demo, pricing, free trial | Conversion | "Start Your Free Trial" |
| Retention | Help docs, tutorials, updates | Activation | "Getting Started Guide" |

### 4. Content Calendar

**Minimum cadence:** 2-4 posts per month

**Weekly schedule example:**
- Monday: Plan + outline
- Tuesday-Wednesday: Draft
- Thursday: Edit + optimize
- Friday: Publish + distribute

### 5. Distribution Channels
- Owned: Blog, email, social media
- Earned: PR, guest posts, backlinks, mentions
- Paid: Promoted posts, paid social, sponsored content
- Community: Reddit, Hacker News, industry forums, Slack groups

**The 80/20 rule:** Spend 20% of time creating, 80% distributing. Great content with no distribution is wasted effort.

## Keyword Research Process

1. **Seed keywords:** What would your customer search for?
2. **Expand:** Use tools (Ahrefs, SEMrush, Google Suggest) to find related terms
3. **Cluster:** Group keywords by topic (one page per cluster)
4. **Prioritize:** Score by Volume × Intent × Difficulty
5. **Map:** Assign clusters to content pieces and funnel stages

## Content Repurposing
One piece of content → Multiple formats:
- Blog post → Twitter thread → LinkedIn carousel → Email newsletter → YouTube video → Podcast episode

## Programmatic SEO
Template-driven pages at scale:
- "[Product] for [Industry]"
- "[Product] vs [Competitor]"
- "[Product] alternatives"
- "[Topic] template"
- "[Tool] integration"
