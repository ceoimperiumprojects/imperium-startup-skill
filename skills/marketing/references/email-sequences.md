# Email Sequence Templates

## Welcome Sequence (5 emails, SaaS)

### Email 1: Welcome + Quick Win (Day 0, immediate)
```
Subject: You're in — here's your first step

Welcome to [Product]!

Most new users see results within [timeframe] by doing [one specific action].

Here's how: [Link to quick-start guide]

Need help? Reply to this email — I read every one.

[Name], [Title]
```

### Email 2: Key Feature Highlight (Day 1)
```
Subject: The feature most people miss

Most [Product] users don't discover [feature] until week 2.

But it's actually the fastest way to [benefit].

Here's a 2-minute walkthrough: [Link]

Already using it? Reply and let me know how it's going.
```

### Email 3: Social Proof (Day 3)
```
Subject: How [Customer] got [result]

[Customer] was struggling with [problem] — just like you might be.

After [timeframe] with [Product], they [specific result].

Here's what they did differently: [Link to case study]
```

### Email 4: Value Content (Day 5)
```
Subject: [Number] tips for [outcome]

I put together [number] tips that our most successful users follow:

1. [Tip 1]
2. [Tip 2]
3. [Tip 3]

Full list here: [Link]
```

### Email 5: Soft Nudge (Day 7)
```
Subject: Quick question

I noticed you [haven't completed X / haven't tried Y].

Is there something blocking you? Seriously — I want to help.

[If on free plan: Our [plan name] plan includes [key feature]. Here's what you'd get: [Link]]
```

## Nurture Sequence (Ongoing, for non-converters)
- 80% value / 20% promotional ratio
- Weekly or bi-weekly cadence
- Segment by engagement level
- Mix content types: tips, case studies, industry news, product updates
- Re-segment based on opens/clicks

## Re-engagement Sequence (4 emails)

### Email 1: "We miss you" (Day 30 inactive)
```
Subject: still there?

Haven't seen you in [Product] lately.

Just want to check — is everything ok? Did you run into an issue?

Reply and let me know. I'm here to help.
```

### Email 2: "What's new" (Day 37)
```
Subject: [number] things that changed since you left

We've been busy. Here's what's new:

• [New feature 1]
• [New feature 2]
• [Improvement]

Worth another look? [Link]
```

### Email 3: "Incentive" (Day 44)
```
Subject: [X]% off — just for you

I want to make this easy.

Here's [X]% off [plan] for the next [timeframe]: [Code/Link]

No pressure — just wanted you to know the option's there.
```

### Email 4: "Sunset" (Day 51)
```
Subject: should I remove you?

I don't want to keep emailing if [Product] isn't for you.

If you want to stay on the list, just click here: [Stay link]

Otherwise, I'll remove you in [X] days. No hard feelings.
```

## Email Best Practices
- Subject lines: Short (3-7 words), specific, curiosity-driven
- Preview text: Extends the subject (don't waste it)
- One CTA per email
- Mobile-first (60%+ open on mobile)
- Plain text often outperforms HTML for B2B
- Send time: Test, but Tue-Thu mornings often win
- Always include unsubscribe link
- Track: Open rate (20-30%), Click rate (2-5%), Conversion rate, Unsubscribe rate (<0.5%)
