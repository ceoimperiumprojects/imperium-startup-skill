# Social Media Strategy

## Platform Priorities

| Business Type | Primary | Secondary | Tertiary |
|--------------|---------|-----------|----------|
| B2B SaaS | LinkedIn | Twitter/X | YouTube |
| B2C App | Instagram | TikTok | Twitter/X |
| Dev Tools | Twitter/X | GitHub | YouTube, Reddit |
| E-commerce | Instagram | TikTok | Pinterest |

## Content Mix (4:1:1 Rule)
- 4 value/educational posts (tips, insights, how-tos)
- 1 soft promotion (product update, case study)
- 1 community/engagement (question, poll, UGC)

## LinkedIn Strategy (B2B)

### Post Types That Work
1. **Personal stories** with business lessons (highest engagement)
2. **Contrarian takes** on industry topics
3. **How-to threads** with specific steps
4. **Data/insights** from your experience
5. **Behind-the-scenes** of building your company

### LinkedIn Post Format
```
Hook line (stops the scroll)

↓ Skip a line

Short paragraph expanding on the hook.

Then bullet points:
• Point 1
• Point 2
• Point 3

Call to action or question at the end.
```

### LinkedIn Best Practices
- Post 3-5x/week
- Best times: Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM
- Engage in comments for 30 min after posting
- No external links in post body (put in comments)
- Use first-person stories
- 1200-1500 characters optimal length

## Twitter/X Strategy

### Post Types
1. **Threads** (step-by-step, story arcs)
2. **Hot takes** (contrarian but substantive)
3. **Tips/tricks** (quick value)
4. **Screenshots** (results, dashboards, before/after)
5. **Engagement** (replies to relevant conversations)

### Best Practices
- Tweet 2-5x/day
- Reply to 10-20 relevant tweets/day
- Use threads for longer content (start with a hook)
- No more than 1-2 hashtags (or none)
- Visual content performs 2x better

## Engagement Strategy

### The 30-Minute Daily Routine
1. **10 min:** Post your content
2. **10 min:** Engage with ICP accounts (thoughtful comments, not "Great post!")
3. **10 min:** Reply to your own post's comments

### Community Building
- Join relevant Slack/Discord communities
- Provide value before promoting
- Share insights, answer questions
- Become a recognized helpful presence
- Only mention your product when genuinely relevant
