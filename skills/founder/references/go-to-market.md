# Go-to-Market Planning

## First 100 Customers Playbook

### Channel Selection
| Channel | Effort | Time to Results | Best For |
|---------|--------|----------------|----------|
| Personal network | Low | Immediate | Any B2B |
| Cold outreach | Medium | 2-4 weeks | B2B with clear ICP |
| Communities | Medium | 2-8 weeks | Niche products |
| Product Hunt | Low | 1 day | B2C, dev tools |
| Content/SEO | High | 3-6 months | Long-term play |
| Paid ads | Medium | 1-2 weeks | B2C, validated value prop |
| Partnerships | Medium | 2-4 weeks | Complementary products |

### The ICP-First Approach
1. Define your ICP with surgical precision
2. Find 100 people who match
3. Reach out personally (no automation for first 100)
4. Learn from every conversation
5. Iterate message and product based on feedback

## Sales Motion Selection

| ACV (Annual Contract Value) | Sales Motion | Team |
|----------------------------|-------------|------|
| <$1K | Self-serve (PLG) | No salespeople needed |
| $1K-10K | Sales-assisted | Founder selling, then 1 AE |
| $10K-50K | Inside sales | SDR + AE team |
| $50K+ | Enterprise sales | SDR + AE + SE + CS |

## Launch Checklist
- [ ] ICP defined (specific, documented)
- [ ] Positioning statement written and tested
- [ ] Landing page live with clear CTA
- [ ] Payment/signup flow working end-to-end
- [ ] 3 channels identified and prioritized for first 100
- [ ] Success metrics defined with targets
- [ ] Support process ready (even if it's just email)
- [ ] Onboarding flow (even if manual)
- [ ] Analytics tracking core events

## Post-Launch: Reading the Signals

| Signal | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| High signup, low activation | Value prop works, product doesn't | Fix onboarding/UX |
| Low signup | Positioning or distribution problem | Test new messaging/channels |
| High activation, low retention | Initial value but no lasting need | Deepen product, add features |
| Organic word-of-mouth | Product-market fit signals | Double down, scale |
| Lots of feature requests | Users engaged, product gaps | Prioritize and ship |
