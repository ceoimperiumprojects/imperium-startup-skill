# Video Types Reference

Complete reference for all 5 startup video templates with full scripts, dimensions, platform guidance, and distribution strategy.

---

## 1. Pitch Video

### Full Script Template

```
SHOT 1 — HOOK (0:00–0:05)
[Full-screen animated text on brand background]
Text: "{Surprising statistic or bold claim about the problem}"
Narration: None — let the text breathe
Transition: Quick fade to black, then cut

SHOT 2 — PROBLEM (0:05–0:20)
[Animated icons or illustrations depicting the problem]
Text overlay: Key pain point keywords
Narration: "Every day, {target audience} struggles with {problem}.
They spend {time/money} on {current solution} — and it's still broken."
Transition: Slide left

SHOT 3 — SOLUTION (0:20–0:40)
[Product logo reveal → product interface screenshots]
Text overlay: Product name + one-line value prop
Narration: "{Product} makes {outcome} effortless.
Instead of {old way}, you simply {new way}."
Transition: Morph/zoom into demo

SHOT 4 — DEMO (0:40–1:00)
[Screen recording with animated callouts and zoom effects]
Text overlay: Feature names as they appear
Narration: "Watch how {user persona} uses {product} to {achieve outcome}.
Step one: {action}. Step two: {action}. Done."
Transition: Fade to traction slide

SHOT 5 — TRACTION (1:00–1:10)
[Animated counters and metric cards]
Text overlay: 3 key metrics with numbers counting up
Narration: "{X} users. {Y} in revenue. {Z}% month-over-month growth."
Transition: Scale up to CTA

SHOT 6 — CTA (1:10–1:20)
[Brand logo centered, URL below, optional QR code]
Text overlay: Clear action — "Start free at {url}"
Narration: "Join {X} teams already using {product}. Start free at {url}."
Transition: Fade to black
```

### Dimensions & Specs
- **Primary format:** 1920x1080 (16:9) for website, YouTube, pitch meetings
- **Secondary format:** 1080x1080 (1:1) for LinkedIn feed
- **FPS:** 30fps standard
- **Duration:** 60–90 seconds (strict — investors lose attention after 90s)
- **File format:** MP4 (H.264 codec, CRF 18 for quality)
- **File size target:** Under 50MB for easy sharing

### Music & Audio
- **Mood:** Inspirational, forward-moving, builds momentum
- **BPM:** 100–120 BPM
- **Arc:** Subtle intro → builds during problem/solution → peaks at traction → resolves at CTA
- **Sources:** Artlist (search "startup", "innovation"), Epidemic Sound, Musicbed
- **Volume:** Music at -20dB under narration, -10dB during text-only sections

### Distribution Strategy
| Platform | Format | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Website hero | 16:9 | Autoplay muted, captions essential |
| YouTube | 16:9 | Add end screen with subscribe CTA |
| LinkedIn | 1:1 or 16:9 | Native upload, add captions |
| Email to investors | 16:9 | Host on Loom or YouTube (unlisted), share link |
| Pitch meeting | 16:9 | Play from local file, no buffering risk |

## 2. Product Demo

### Full Script Template

```
SHOT 1 — CONTEXT (0:00–0:15)
[Title card with product logo and tagline]
Text: "{Product}: {One-line description}"
Narration: "If you're a {persona} who needs to {goal},
this is how {product} makes it happen."
Transition: Dissolve to screen recording

SHOT 2 — FEATURE 1: {Core Feature} (0:15–0:45)
[Screen recording: show the workflow step by step]
Text overlay: Feature name + benefit callout
Narration: "First, let's look at {feature}.
Here's a real example: {use case scenario}.
Notice how {benefit} — that's {time/effort} saved every single time."
Visual: Cursor highlights, zoom on key UI elements, annotation arrows
Transition: Slide right to Feature 2

SHOT 3 — FEATURE 2: {Key Feature} (0:45–1:15)
[Screen recording: different workflow or capability]
Text overlay: Feature name + benefit callout
Narration: "Next, {feature}. This is where {product} really shines.
{Persona} told us they used to spend {time} on this. Now it takes {less time}."
Visual: Split screen showing before/after when applicable
Transition: Slide right to Feature 3

SHOT 4 — FEATURE 3: {Differentiating Feature} (1:15–1:45)
[Screen recording: unique capability or integration]
Text overlay: Feature name + benefit callout
Narration: "And here's what sets {product} apart: {feature}.
Unlike {competitor approach}, we {unique approach}."
Visual: Animated comparison or workflow diagram
Transition: Zoom out to summary

SHOT 5 — SUMMARY (1:45–2:00)
[Clean slide with 3 benefit bullets]
Text: Three key benefits as animated bullet points
Narration: "So with {product}, you get:
{Benefit 1}. {Benefit 2}. And {benefit 3}."
Transition: Fade to CTA

SHOT 6 — CTA (2:00–2:10)
[Brand logo, URL, action button animation]
Text: "Try {product} free — {url}"
Narration: "Start your free trial at {url}. Setup takes under {time}."
Transition: Fade to black
```

### Dimensions & Specs
- **Primary format:** 1920x1080 (16:9)
- **FPS:** 30fps (60fps if showing smooth UI animations)
- **Duration:** 2–3 minutes (shorter is always better)
- **Screen recording:** Capture at 2x resolution, scale down for crisp text
- **Cursor:** Use a visible, branded cursor highlight (yellow circle or similar)
- **File format:** MP4, H.264, CRF 16 (higher quality for text-heavy content)

### Music & Audio
- **Mood:** Upbeat, modern, steady energy — not distracting
- **BPM:** 110–130 BPM
- **Arc:** Consistent energy throughout, slight lift at summary
- **Volume:** -22dB under narration (lower than pitch video — more talking here)
- **SFX:** Subtle click/whoosh sounds for UI interactions (optional)

### Distribution Strategy
| Platform | Format | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Website /demo page | 16:9 | Embed with chapters/timestamps |
| YouTube | 16:9 | Add chapters in description, cards for features |
| Sales emails | 16:9 | Host on Loom, share with thumbnail |
| Onboarding | 16:9 | Break into feature-specific clips |
| Help center | 16:9 | Individual feature segments |

## 3. Social Clip

### Full Script Template

```
SHOT 1 — HOOK (0:00–0:03)
[Bold text animation, high contrast, fills the screen]
Text: "{Provocative question or surprising stat}"
Example: "90% of startups get pricing wrong"
Visual: Text slams in with motion blur or typewriter effect
Audio: Beat drop or attention sound

SHOT 2 — VALUE (0:03–0:20)
[Quick cuts between text cards, product shots, or talking-head style]
Text overlay: Key points as large, readable text
Content options:
  A) One tip/insight with 3 supporting points
  B) Before/after comparison
  C) Quick product feature showcase
  D) Hot take with evidence
Visual: Dynamic — zoom, pan, text animations every 2-3 seconds
Audio: Background beat, text sounds on key words

SHOT 3 — CTA (0:20–0:25)
[Brand logo + action]
Text: "Follow for more" / "Link in bio" / "Try it free"
Visual: Logo animation, profile tag, or URL
Audio: Music resolves
```

### Dimensions & Specs

**Vertical (9:16) — TikTok, Reels, Shorts:**
- Resolution: 1080x1920
- Safe zone: Keep text within center 900x1400 (platform UI covers edges)
- FPS: 30fps
- Duration: 15–30 seconds (sweet spot: 18–22 seconds)
- Captions: Bottom-center, large font (minimum 48px at 1080 width)

**Square (1:1) — LinkedIn, Instagram Feed:**
- Resolution: 1080x1080
- FPS: 30fps
- Duration: 15–45 seconds (LinkedIn allows longer engagement)
- Captions: Bottom-center or top-center

**Horizontal (16:9) — Twitter/X, YouTube:**
- Resolution: 1920x1080
- FPS: 30fps
- Duration: 15–60 seconds
- Captions: Bottom-center

### Music & Audio
- **Mood:** Trending, energetic, beat-driven
- **BPM:** 120–140 BPM
- **Key:** Use trending sounds when possible (check TikTok Creative Center)
- **Volume:** Music is louder here than other formats — it drives the energy
- **SFX:** Whoosh on transitions, pop on text reveals, bass hit on hook

### Distribution Strategy
| Platform | Format | Max Duration | Best Posting Time |
|----------|--------|-------------|-------------------|
| TikTok | 9:16 | 60s (but 15-22s performs best) | 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-11 PM |
| Instagram Reels | 9:16 | 90s (but 15-22s performs best) | 9 AM, 12 PM, 6 PM |
| YouTube Shorts | 9:16 | 60s | 12-3 PM, 7-9 PM |
| LinkedIn | 1:1 or 16:9 | 10 min (but 30-60s performs best) | Tue-Thu 8-10 AM |
| Twitter/X | 16:9 | 2:20 | 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM |

## 4. Investor Update Video

### Full Script Template

```
SHOT 1 — KEY METRIC (0:00–0:10)
[Dark background, single large number animates in]
Text: "{Metric name}: {Number}" with growth indicator (↑ X%)
Narration: "This month, we hit {metric} — up {X}% from last month."
Visual: Number counts up from 0 to target, growth arrow animates
Transition: Fade to milestones

SHOT 2 — MILESTONES (0:10–0:30)
[Timeline or card layout, items animate in sequence]
Text: 3-5 milestone cards with dates
Narration: "Here's what we shipped:
{Milestone 1} — {date and impact}
{Milestone 2} — {date and impact}
{Milestone 3} — {date and impact}"
Visual: Cards slide in from right, checkmarks appear
Transition: Subtle shift to challenges

SHOT 3 — CHALLENGES (0:30–0:40)
[Clean, honest layout — no dramatic visuals]
Text: 1-2 challenge statements with "Addressing by..." action
Narration: "We're navigating {challenge}. Our approach: {solution}.
We expect to resolve this by {timeline}."
Visual: Challenge text with arrow pointing to solution text
Transition: Slide to next quarter

SHOT 4 — NEXT QUARTER (0:40–0:55)
[Numbered priority list with icons]
Text: Top 3 priorities as numbered items
Narration: "Our top three priorities for next quarter:
One: {priority} — targeting {measurable goal}
Two: {priority} — targeting {measurable goal}
Three: {priority} — targeting {measurable goal}"
Visual: Numbers and icons animate in sequence
Transition: Fade to ask

SHOT 5 — ASK (0:55–1:00)
[Direct, personal feel]
Text: Specific ask + contact method
Narration: "If you can help with {specific request}, I'd love to connect.
Reply to this email or grab time at {calendar link}."
Visual: Email/calendar link displayed clearly
Transition: Fade to logo, then black
```

### Dimensions & Specs
- **Primary format:** 1920x1080 (16:9)
- **FPS:** 30fps
- **Duration:** 45–75 seconds (respect investor time)
- **File format:** MP4, H.264
- **Style:** Data-forward, clean, minimal animations
- **Charts:** If using charts, animate data points in — don't show static charts

### Music & Audio
- **Mood:** Subtle, ambient, professional — this is a business update, not a hype reel
- **BPM:** 80–100 BPM
- **Volume:** Very low (-25dB under narration) — almost atmospheric
- **Alternative:** No music at all is also appropriate for updates

### Distribution Strategy
| Platform | Format | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Email to investors | 16:9 | Host on Loom (unlisted), embed thumbnail in email |
| Board portal | 16:9 | Upload directly to your investor portal |
| Internal team | 16:9 | Share in Slack/Teams for alignment |
| LinkedIn (selective) | 16:9 | Only share if comfortable with public traction data |

## 5. Team / Hiring Video

### Full Script Template

```
SHOT 1 — CULTURE HOOK (0:00–0:05)
[Energetic opening — bold text or team moment]
Text: "{One line that captures your team's energy}"
Examples:
  "We ship on Fridays and we like it."
  "12 people. 3 countries. One mission."
  "We broke prod twice this month. We also 10x'd revenue."
Narration: None — text only for punch
Visual: Text animation with team photo background or brand colors
Transition: Quick cut

SHOT 2 — WHAT WE BUILD (0:05–0:20)
[Product shots mixed with impact visuals]
Text overlay: Mission statement + product category
Narration: "We're building {product} — {one-line mission}.
{Impact statement}: {who benefits and how}."
Visual: Product interface shots, user testimonials (text), impact metrics
Transition: Energetic cut

SHOT 3 — TEAM VIBE (0:20–0:35)
[Candid team content — photos, chat screenshots, workspace]
Text overlay: Team values or work style keywords
Narration: "How we work: {work style}.
We believe in {value 1}, {value 2}, and {value 3}.
{Specific example of culture — a ritual, tradition, or story}."
Visual: Team photos, Slack screenshots, office/remote workspace shots
Transition: Slide to role

SHOT 4 — THE ROLE (0:35–0:50)
[Clean role card with key details]
Text: Role title + 3 key responsibilities/impact areas
Narration: "We're looking for a {role title}.
You'll {impact 1}, {impact 2}, and {impact 3}.
{What makes this role special or unique}."
Visual: Role card animating in with bullet points
Transition: Fade to CTA

SHOT 5 — APPLY CTA (0:50–0:55)
[Brand logo + careers URL]
Text: "Apply at {careers-url}" or "DM us"
Narration: "Interested? Apply at {url}. We read every application."
Visual: Logo + URL + optional QR code
Transition: Fade to black
```

### Dimensions & Specs
- **Primary format:** 1080x1920 (9:16) for social recruiting
- **Secondary format:** 1920x1080 (16:9) for careers page
- **FPS:** 30fps
- **Duration:** 30–60 seconds
- **Style:** Authentic over polished — real photos beat stock imagery

### Music & Audio
- **Mood:** Energetic, uplifting, matches team personality
- **BPM:** 120–140 BPM
- **Style:** Choose music that reflects actual team culture:
  - Tech/startup: Electronic, lo-fi, indie
  - Creative agency: Bold, eclectic
  - Enterprise: Modern corporate, subtle
- **Volume:** Can be more prominent than other formats — energy matters here

### Distribution Strategy
| Platform | Format | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| LinkedIn (company page) | 16:9 | Pin to top, add job link in comments |
| LinkedIn (team members) | 9:16 or 1:1 | Personal posts get 5-10x more reach |
| Instagram/TikTok | 9:16 | Tag candidates, use hiring hashtags |
| Careers page | 16:9 | Embed above job listings |
| Job boards | 16:9 | Attach to listing when supported |
| Recruiting emails | 16:9 | Loom link with thumbnail |

## When to Use Each Type — Decision Guide

| Situation | Recommended Video | Why |
|-----------|-------------------|-----|
| Fundraising | Pitch Video | Concise, metric-driven, shows product |
| Website launch | Product Demo | Converts visitors who want to understand the product |
| Growing social presence | Social Clip | Algorithm-friendly, shareable, builds brand awareness |
| Investor relations | Investor Update | Professional, respectful of time, shows transparency |
| Hiring push | Hiring Video | Attracts culture-fit candidates, humanizes the company |
| Conference talk | Product Demo + Social Clips | Demo for the booth, clips for social promotion |
| Product Hunt launch | Pitch Video (shortened) | 60s version with strong hook and demo |
| Sales outreach | Product Demo (shortened) | 60-90s version focused on prospect's use case |

## Platform-Specific Best Practices

### YouTube
- Add chapters with timestamps in description
- Custom thumbnail (don't use auto-generated)
- Include cards and end screens
- First 30 seconds determine if people stay — front-load value

### LinkedIn
- Native upload always (never YouTube links — algorithm penalizes)
- Add captions (85% of LinkedIn video is watched without sound)
- Square (1:1) gets 78% more real estate in feed than 16:9
- Post text should add context, not repeat the video

### TikTok / Reels
- First 2 seconds are everything — hook immediately
- Use trending sounds when relevant
- Text overlays are mandatory
- Vertical only — never post horizontal content
- Loop potential: if the video loops smoothly, watch time increases

### Twitter/X
- Auto-plays muted — captions are essential
- Keep under 45 seconds for best engagement
- Tweet text should drive curiosity to watch
- Pin important videos to profile
