### 3. Unique Value Proposition
Single, clear, compelling message that states why you are different and worth paying attention to.

**Formula:** Clear outcome + specific timeframe + key differentiator
**Example:** "Get 3x more qualified leads in 30 days without cold calling"

**High-Level Concept:** X for Y analogy (e.g., "Uber for dog walking")

### 8. Key Metrics
What numbers tell you the business is working?
- **Activation:** What % of signups reach the "aha moment"?
- **Retention:** What % come back after Day 1 / Day 7 / Day 30?
- **Revenue:** MRR, ARPU, growth rate

### 9. Unfair Advantage
Something that cannot be easily copied or bought.
- Insider information / domain expertise
- Network effects
- Community / audience
- Proprietary technology / data
- Team with unique experience
- Existing customers / distribution

## How to Use

1. **Fill it out in 20 minutes** — first pass should be fast and messy
2. **Identify riskiest assumptions** — what must be true for this to work?
3. **Test those assumptions first** — before building anything
4. **Update weekly** — this is a living document, not a one-time exercise
5. **One canvas per customer segment** — if you have multiple segments, make multiple canvases
